GAME RULES

1. Players take turns rolling a six-sided die.

2. Wait for your next turn.

Chicken (aka. ladder): If you lands on a "Chicken" (a picture of a chicken on the board), you immediately move your piece to the "Muscle chicken" land.


Rotten food (aka. snake): If you lands on a "Rotten food" (a picture of a rotten food on the board), you immediately drop your piece down to the "Toilet" land.


How to win? : The first player to reach or exactly reach the final square wins the game.
